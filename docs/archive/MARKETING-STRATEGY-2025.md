# OnCallShift Marketing Strategy 2025
## Comprehensive Website Positioning & Messaging

**Last Updated:** January 2, 2025
**Purpose:** Transform OnCallShift's website to clearly communicate our unique AI-first incident response platform

---

## Executive Summary

**The Challenge:** Our current website positions us as "another PagerDuty alternative" when we're actually building something fundamentally different: the first AI-native incident response platform with execution capabilities.

**The Opportunity:** We have technical capabilities (AI with tool use, mobile-first, cloud investigation, BYOK model) that competitors can't match, but we're not communicating this clearly.

**The Authenticity Factor:** We're DevOps engineers who got tired of shitty on-call tools. We built the platform we wish we had when we were getting paged at 3am. This isn't enterprise software built by product managers—it's battle-tested tooling built by people who live on-call.

**The Solution:** Reposition from "on-call scheduling tool" to "AI incident response platform" with three pillars:
1. **Smart Routing** - Get the right person instantly
2. **AI Resolution** - Fix problems from your phone in 60 seconds
3. **Transparent Pricing** - $0 to start, no sales calls required (because we hate sales calls too)
